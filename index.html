About Me

Hi! I'm a software developer based in Vancouver, BC. I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics at UBC. Previously, I worked as a co-op software developer at Hootsuite and at SAP.

My greatest programming interests lie in web development, utilizing JavaScript, HTML, CSS, as well as other frameworks and libraries such as React and Vue.js to implement beautiful and intuitive web interfaces. In addition to my co-op experiences that both made use of these technologies, I worked on several academic and personal projects through which I have elevated my skills.

I also enjoy programming in Java, which was the first official programming language I had worked with. I am also interested in learning more about mobile development; I worked on some projects with React Native, and also with Java and XML through Android Studio.

Outside of my programming life, I have a passion for music and playing instruments. I started playing piano since I was young, and picked up guitar a couple years ago as well. I enjoy both playing alone or as part of a band. Occasionally I am also a hobbyist photographer.
